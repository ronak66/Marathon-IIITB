About the event

Introducing RUN BHOOMI IIITB's First open “Run for Lake” . 10 years ago Bengaluru had numerous lakes, but we hardly see any lakes around now, and the few we see are far beyond polluted. With the growth of IT industry here, the need for more housing and rapid urbanization have resulted in reckless exploitation of the sorroundings and natural resources. Many lakes in Bengaluru have either been neglected or encroached and destroyed. It is high time we do something, else lakes in Bengaluru will be completly extinct. So IIITB, along with ELCITA has taken up the initiative to run a campaign to protect and revive the lakes. We plan to raise funds to revive Doddathogur Lake in Konappana Agrahara. This “Run for Lake” is a call to all of the citizens around Bengaluru. This is a chance to protect Bengaluru and rid it of the menace of reckless exploitation of our lakes. We call upon as much participation as possible for this yeomen cause. Join us on April 13th, Saturday,  @IIITB (start point- in front of Infosys Gate No2)

6 Km Marathon

Marathon held by IIIT-Bangalore with the purpose of saving and reviving Doddathogur Lake.
